Architect Day: Gracia Studio
Gracia Studio is an office based in San Diego, but with great performance in Mexico, where they have projects in the residential and commercial area. Formed in 2005, the studio shows an interesting variety of projects. graciastudio.com Born in 1973 and 1983, respectively, Jorge and Javier Gracia received their degree from the Universidad Iberoamericana in Mexico. Founded in 2005, the office was called Gracia Studio. With a contemporary architecture, but with Mexican roots present, they have interesting projects both residential and commercial. Born in America in 1929, Ralph was the hand behind the concept art that helped bring life to some of the most famous characters of all times, like Darth Vader and Yoda. Of course that seeing these you'll get to see that the final product, the one we watched on screen, was slightly different from Ralph's concepts, which are great nonetheless.
